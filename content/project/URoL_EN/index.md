NSF-funded URoL:EN project (EF-2133863), developing a unified theory of regulatory functions and networks across biological and social systems. As part of this project, we built a century-scale dataset of the U.S. legal code and analyzed the evolution of its complexity (Jeong et al., *Scientific Data*, 2026).
